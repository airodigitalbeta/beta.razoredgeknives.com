How do we Sharpen?

It’s simple: on powered, and non-powered equipment. But the key is that when we sharpen on powered equipment, it is done using Kool Mist units so that way your blade is under a constant water mist to prevent temper damage!
 
We utilize the Wicked Edge Precision Sharpener when sharpening by hand, and this system offers the ultimate in performance. All high dollar customs are sharpened on this system!
